Ecommerce Case Study: ArcadeStickLabs

How a small ecommerce brand moved from manual sales to a fast, conversion-focused online store built for repeat customers and growth.

Visit Live Store

The Challenge

ArcadeStickLabs started as a passion-driven brand but quickly hit limitations with manual ordering, slow checkout flow and product management friction.

Off-the-shelf ecommerce setups struggled with product variants, bundles and repeat purchasing. This created unnecessary admin work and limited the brand’s ability to scale efficiently.

The Solution

  • Custom-built ecommerce storefront focused on speed & UX
  • Flexible product variants and bundle logic
  • Optimised checkout with Stripe payments
  • Admin tooling to simplify order fulfilment
  • SEO-friendly product and category pages

The Results

  • Smoother checkout experience
  • Reduced manual admin work
  • Faster repeat ordering for returning customers
  • Scalable store architecture for new product launches

Who This Is For

This approach is ideal for small ecommerce brands that have outgrown basic templates and want a faster, cleaner store that actually converts visitors into customers.
