Case Study - Talibah (Muslim Matchmaker)

A custom-built Muslim marriage platform designed to replace chaotic, unsafe matchmaking with a structured, halal, and scalable SaaS system.

The Problem

Muslim marriage seekers were relying on WhatsApp groups, spreadsheets, and unsafe dating apps that did not align with Islamic values, lacked moderation, and provided no serious filtering, privacy or long-term structure.

The Solution

  • Custom-built SaaS matchmaking engine
  • Faith-aligned onboarding and verification flow
  • Structured halal messaging & conversation gating
  • Subscription-based monetisation
  • Admin moderation console
  • Advanced filtering and compatibility logic
  • Secure authentication & GDPR-compliant data handling

The Result

  • High conversion subscription funnel
  • Structured onboarding reduced ghosting and misuse
  • Fully scalable SaaS infrastructure
  • Clear monetisation model
  • Production-ready system capable of mass rollout

Who This Is For

Ideal for founders building niche SaaS platforms, faith-based communities, subscription platforms, or regulated matchmaking services that require structured onboarding, moderation, and monetisation.
